Dog Park Policy Changes

The Dog Park was never intended for public use by dogs or humans, and even acknowledging the existence of The Dog Park is illegal. But, after the recent outcry, the City Council has decided to make a policy change.

Night Vale citizens can now apply to let Hooded Figures play with their dog in The Dog Park. However, the dog owners will be packed away in styrofoam containers for the duration.
